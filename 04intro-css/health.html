Easy daily changes

Eat a Balanced Diet

  • Include more fruits and vegetables in your meals.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • Limit processed foods and added sugars.

Stay Hydrated

  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water per day.
  • Avoid sugary drinks and excessive caffeine.
  • Hydration supports metabolism and energy levels.

Exercise Regularly

  • Aim for at least 30 minutes of activity most days.
  • Include both cardio and strength training.
  • Find an activity you enjoy to stay consistent.

Get Enough Sleep

  • Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.
  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule.
  • Limit screen time before bed to improve sleep quality.

Manage Stress

  • Practice meditation, deep breathing, or yoga.
  • Take breaks and engage in hobbies you enjoy.
  • Stay connected with family and friends for support.

Reduce Sedentary Time

  • Take short breaks from sitting every hour.
  • Incorporate walking meetings or standing desks.
  • Stay active throughout the day, even with small movements.

Maintain Mental Well-being

  • Practice gratitude and positive thinking.
  • Engage in activities that stimulate your mind.
  • Seek professional help if you experience persistent stress or anxiety.
